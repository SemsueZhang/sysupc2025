# sysupc2025
2025 Sun Yat-sen University Programming Contest.

## About the Contest

The Sun Yat-sen University Programming Contest (SYSUPC) is an annual competitive programming competition that brings together talented students to solve challenging algorithmic problems. The contest serves as a platform for students to demonstrate their problem-solving skills, algorithmic thinking, and programming expertise.

### Aims

The primary aims of SYSUPC are to:
- Foster algorithmic thinking and problem-solving skills among students
- Encourage collaboration and friendly competition in the programming community
- Provide a platform for students to practice and improve their competitive programming abilities
- Identify and nurture talented programmers
- Prepare students for international programming competitions such as ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest)

### History

SYSUPC has been a significant event in Sun Yat-sen University's academic calendar, attracting participants from various departments and skill levels. The contest has grown over the years, contributing to the university's strong reputation in computer science education and competitive programming.
